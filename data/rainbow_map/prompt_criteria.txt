Base Template Structure:
[Agent] should [Action] [Ground/Target] [Context/Domain]

Where:
•	[Agent] = Who is responsible (e.g., “The Constitution”, “National law”, “Legislation”, “Equality body”, “Healthcare system”)
•	[Action]:
•	Pro: explicitly prohibit discrimination based on, explicitly include, recognize, protect, ban, etc.
•	Con: allow for discrimination based on, exclude, fail to recognize, permit, not be required to address, etc.
•	[Ground/Target] = What the action targets (e.g., sexual orientation, conversion practices, LGBT people, etc.)
•	[Context/Domain] = In which area or under what conditions (e.g., in employment, in health services, for minors, in constitutional law)
